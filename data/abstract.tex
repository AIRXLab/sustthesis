
毕业论文题目

\begin{abstract}
摘要是学位论文的重要组成部分，是对论文研究工作的全面、准确和高度凝练的概括。摘要应简要阐述研究工作的背景与目的，说明所采用的主要研究方法，概括论文的主要研究内容、关键成果及研究结论，突出体现论文的创新性、科学性和应用价值。摘要具有独立性和完整性，读者无需查阅论文正文即可基本了解论文的主要内容。撰写摘要时应做到语言简洁、表述客观、逻辑清晰，避免出现与研究内容无关的描述、过程性细节以及主观评价，使其能够有效反映学位论文的核心思想和研究成果。

\end{abstract}



\begin{abstract*}

Abstract is an essential component of a thesis, providing a concise, accurate, and comprehensive summary of the research work. It briefly presents the background and objectives of the study, the main research methods, the key research contents, major findings, and conclusions, highlighting the scientific significance, innovation, and potential applications of the research. An abstract should be independent and self-contained, allowing readers to understand the main ideas and contributions of the thesis without referring to the full text. It should be written in a clear, concise, and objective manner, avoiding unnecessary details, subjective evaluations, and irrelevant descriptions, so as to effectively convey the core content and achievements of the research.

\end{abstract*}
