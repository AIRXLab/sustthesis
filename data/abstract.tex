\begin{abstract*}

Abstract is an essential component of a thesis, providing a concise, accurate, and comprehensive summary of the research work. It briefly presents the background and objectives of the study, the main research methods, the key research contents, major findings, and conclusions, highlighting the scientific significance, innovation, and potential applications of the research. An abstract should be independent and self-contained, allowing readers to understand the main ideas and contributions of the thesis without referring to the full text. It should be written in a clear, concise, and objective manner, avoiding unnecessary details, subjective evaluations, and irrelevant descriptions, to effectively convey the core content and achievements of the research.

\end{abstract*}
